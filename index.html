Welcome to British Scouting Overseas Dubai

British Scouting Overseas (BSO) Dubai provides fun and friendship, challenge and everyday adventure to over 200 young people in Dubai, for both boys and girls aged 4 to 18.

Joining us couldn't be easier

Register your interest to join BSO Dubai. We welcome everyone to join us. Priority will be given to young people who have a parent willing to volunteer some of their time to help out. No previous experience necessary!

If you would like to both volunteer and register a Young Person, please ensure you complete both entry forms on this page. 

Join Scouts

For each young person you would like to register, fill out an entry form below. Adults above the age of 18 are welcome to volunteer and do not need to have a child in Scouting.
